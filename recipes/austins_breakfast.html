Austin's Famous Breakfast

Description

This is my real, genuine daily breakfast. I eat this at least 5x a week unless I am fasting. Similar to David Lynch, I find consistency in my diet really does free the mind. I'd like to eventually just have 7 copies of the same outfit as well, a la Einstein.

Ingredients

  • 3 large free range eggs
  • 3 turkey sausage links
  • 1 cup of greek yogurt
  • 1 apple (pink lady or granny smith recommended)
  • 1 cup of coffee or can of monster

Steps

  1. Place medium saucepan on stove
  2. Add either olive oil or cooking spray to pan and bring to medium heat
  3. Add eggs and turkey saugsage to pan
  4. Cook until eggs are overhard and sausage is brown on both sides
  5. Place cooked eggs on plate
  6. Place greek yogurt and apple on additional plate
  7. Pour caffeinated beverage of choice into mug
  8. Enjoy!
