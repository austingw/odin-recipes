Fried Rice

Description

This is a recipe for Chicken + Bacon Fried Rice. The dish is based on Japanese-style fried rice, but features some tweaks to match an American palette. I highly suggest making a large batch, as it works quite well as "leftovers" when reheated.

Ingredients

  • sushi rice
  • 1lb chicken tenderloin
  • 8 strips bacon
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbl spoons vegetable oil
  • minced garlic
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Add sushi rice and water to pot (follow amount instructions on sushi bag)
  2. Place bacon on baking sheet in oven at 325 degress until crispy
  3. steam frozen vegetables in microwave
  4. add vegetable oil and minced garlic to large pot on medium heat
  5. slice chicken into bite-sized chunksand add to large pot
  6. cook chicken until slightly brown on each side
  7. put pot on low heat
  8. break up bacon once cooked into "bits"
  9. add bacon, vegetables, and rice to large pot once all are cooked
  10. mix chicken, rice, vegetables, and bacon while adding soy sauce to taste
  11. enjoy!
