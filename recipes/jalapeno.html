Austin's Famous Breakfast

Description

Another staple. I love spice. This is a great and easy way to prepare Jalapenos, which offer numerous health benefits. Great alone or with a meal!

Ingredients

  • Jalapeno peppers
  • 1 tbl spoon Olive Oil
  • Adobo seasoning

Steps

  1. Place medium saucepan on stove
  2. Add olive oil and place stove on medium heat
  3. Cut Jalapenos in half
  4. Optional: remove seeds if you are senstitive to spice (not recommended)
  5. Cook Jalapenos on both side until dark
  6. Add Adobo seasoning liberally while peppers are cooking
  7. Enjoy!
